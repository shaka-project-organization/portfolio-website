About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Cloud & DevOps Engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with over 3 years of hands-on experience designing and operating AWS infrastructure at scale. I specialize in Kubernetes orchestration, Infrastructure as Code with Terraform, and building automated CI/CD pipelines that turn code commits into production deployments.

I'm a remote first engineer actively pursuing global opportunities. I thrive in environments where reliability, automation, and fast iteration matter.
